Financial literacy is key to growing a successful business. However, the reality is that many entrepreneurs find themselves at a disadvantage in understanding the basic concepts and language of finance. As a consequence, many struggle with being able to fully understand or communicate the financial health and future direction of their own business. This lack of financial literacy can become a significant barrier to business success. This course was created to help break down this barrier. 
“Financial literacy is having the knowledge, skills and confidence to make responsible financial decisions.”
